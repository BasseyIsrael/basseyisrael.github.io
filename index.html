Get to know me

I am a data analyst with a degree in Petroleum Engineering. I have been working in the capacity as an analyst for 2+ years, seriously bootstraped by my Engineering skillset. I have contributed to various projects including the developments of end to end IOT systems, data-driven automated systems, machine learning models, dynamic statistical models, and database systems.

My passion for knowledge sharing and contribution has also led me to be an instructor and course developer for various data based processes. You can explore my projects, publications, and course materials in this website.
